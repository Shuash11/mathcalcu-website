MathCalcu

Your offline math companion for BSCS — step-by-step, no internet needed.

MathCalcu is a Flutter app built specifically for BSCS students tackling calculus and analytic geometry. It doesn't just give you answers — it walks you through every step, offline, using a built-in symbolic math engine. Whether you're solving derivatives, evaluating limits, or working through circle equations, MathCalcu shows the work the way your professor expects it.

What It Solves

MathCalcu tackles the core topics in calculus and analytic geometry — with full step-by-step solutions.

∂

Derivatives

With full rule identification — power, product, quotient, chain rule and more.

lim

Evaluating Limits

Substitution, factoring, LCD, and conjugate methods for any limit problem.

∞

Limits at Infinity

Rational, radical, and trigonometric limits as x approaches infinity.

📐

Slope Using Derivatives

Explicit, implicit, and parametric differentiation for slope analysis.

≤

Inequalities

Linear, quadratic, rational, radical, and absolute value inequalities.

○

Circles

Find center, radius, standard form and general form equations.

↔

Distance & Midpoint

Coordinate distance and midpoint calculations with graphing support.

/

Slope & Intercept

Point-slope, two-point, parallel and perpendicular line equations.

How It Works

Three simple steps to solve any problem — completely offline.

App Preview — Solving a Derivative

✓ Step 1: Identify rule → Power Rule
✓ Step 2: Apply derivative → 3·2x + 2
✓ Step 3: Simplify → 6x + 2
1

Input Your Problem

Type or paste any math expression — derivatives like d/dx (3x²), limits, equations, and more.

2

Solve Instantly

The built-in symbolic math engine processes your problem in milliseconds — no internet connection required.

Review a complete, professor-ready breakdown showing exactly how each rule is applied to reach the solution.
